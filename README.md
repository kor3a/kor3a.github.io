# Allim
**Allim** (Korean for “alarm”) is a smart location-based reminder app that helps you remember what to do and what to buy when you’re near your favorite stores.
Built for families, roommates, couples, and friends, Allim makes grocery planning and errands collaborative, simple, and reliable.
## Why Allim
- Save your favorite stores in seconds
- Create reminders tied to specific places
- Get nearby alerts so you never miss important errands
- Collaborate on grocery and shopping reminders with people you live with
- Stay connected wherever you are
## Perfect For
- Family grocery planning
- Roommate shopping coordination
- Shared household errands
- Busy schedules and on-the-go reminders
## How It Works
1. Save a store  
2. Add reminders or shopping tasks  
3. Get notified automatically when you’re nearby  
Allim helps you take action at the right place and right time.
